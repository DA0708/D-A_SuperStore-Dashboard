# D-A_SuperStore-Dashboard
PowerBI Dashboard

Executive Summary


D&A Super Store Sales Dashboard offers a highly accurate, multi-page
business intelligence tool tracking long-term retail operations. The dashboard
successfully aggregates data across five major high-level KPIs: $1.57M in Total
Sales, $175.26K in Total Profit, 22K in Total Quantity Sold, an Average Order
Value (AOV) of $70, and a rapid Average Shipping time of 4 days.
By analyzing behavioral segments, operational fulfillment metrics, geographic
distributions, and integrating a dedicated 10-day time-series forecasting engine,
this dashboard provides a comprehensive look at historical performance while
accurately projecting future store demand.


The 30-Second Briefing


➢ The Big Picture: We’ve generated $1.57M in sales with a healthy 11% profit
margin ($175.26K). However, our average shipping time is sitting at 4 days,
largely because 60% of our volume relies on Standard Class shipping. If we
optimize this logistics bottleneck, we can dramatically improve customer
satisfaction and lifetime value.
➢ Where to Focus: Cash on Delivery (COD) is dominating at 43% of our payment
mix, and the Consumer segment is driving nearly half of our revenue (48%).
Geographically, the West and East regions make up over 60% of our market,
anchored heavily by California and New York.
